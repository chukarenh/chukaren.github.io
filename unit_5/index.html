Karen Chu

[MARKETING | DESIGNER | PHOTOGRAPHER]

Karen Chu graduated in 2013 from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) where she majored in Psychology (B.A.) and minored in Film, Television, & Digital Media Studies. She was previously a managing editor, layout designer, and photographer for UCLA's Bruin Life Yearbook and a layout designer for UCLA's Total Wellness Magazine. She enjoys going to music shows, watching movies, cooking/eating, fashion, and taking photographs. She is currently the Marketing Coordinator at Engine Yard and Deis, based out of San Francisco, CA.
